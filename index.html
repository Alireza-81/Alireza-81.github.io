About Me

Hello!

I'm Alireza Amiri, a passionate and motivated Computer Engineering student at Sharif University of Technology. My primary research interests lie in the fields of Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, their interpretability and their applications in creating robust and trustworthy technology.

I have been actively involved in research, particularly in the areas of Natural Language Processing and Vision Language Models. My current research involves working on Adversarial Data Augmentation on Text-to-Image (T2I) models at the L3S Research Center, under the supervision of Prof. Mitra Prasenjit and Dr. Ali Rasekh.

In addition to research, I have a strong foundation in mathematics, as evidenced by my achievements in various national and international Mathematical Olympiads. I received a Silver and Bronze medal in the national Mathematical Olympiad in 2019 and 2020.

I am constantly looking to push the boundaries of my knowledge and skills. My work is a testament to my dedication to advancing the field of Computer Science and my commitment to excellence in all my academic endeavors.
